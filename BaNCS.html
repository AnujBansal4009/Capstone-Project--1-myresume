Project Name : BaNCS Project [Feb 2022 - Current]

BaNCS is a product just like PayPal or any payment gateway site. The Project was to analyze and develop the database and package in such a way that the data shared by the client (which are millions of record) is cleaned and refined on the basis of valid street, city and State. Applied various task and transformations in SQL, SSIS to identify valid and invalid records of US.

Tools used:

  • SSIS
  • SQL
